Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Brain Games: Memory & Focus (the "App") handles information when you use it.

Information we collect

The App is designed to minimize data collection. Game progress, stats, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

To operate in-app purchases and subscriptions, and to diagnose issues, the App and its service providers may process a limited set of non-sensitive information:

  • Pseudonymous identifier — a randomly generated App User ID (derived from Apple's Identifier for Vendor) that is not linked to your real identity.
  • Purchase and subscription data — product identifiers, purchase/renewal dates, trial and refund status, and validated App Store receipts.
  • Device and app information — device model, operating system version, app version, language, and approximate country (derived from IP address at request time).
  • Diagnostic data — anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage metrics used to improve stability and performance.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Third-party services

We use the following service providers, who process the data described above strictly on our behalf to operate and improve the App:

  • Apple — the App Store and StoreKit process your purchases and subscriptions under Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — manages our in-app purchases and subscription entitlements. RevenueCat receives the pseudonymous App User ID, purchase/subscription events, and basic device/app metadata listed above. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for details.

These providers act as data processors and are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes.

Data retention and deletion

Because game data is stored on your device, you can delete all of it at any time by removing the App or using the in-app reset option.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at braingamessupport@gmail.com.
