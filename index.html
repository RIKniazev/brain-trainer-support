Frequently asked questions

How do I reset my progress?

Open the app, go to Settings → Reset progress, then confirm. This clears local stats and cannot be undone.

My progress disappeared after reinstalling. Can I restore it?

Progress is stored on your device. If you deleted the app or switched devices, local stats cannot be recovered. We're working on iCloud sync for a future update.

How do I restore or manage my purchases?

Open the app and tap Settings → Restore purchases. To manage or cancel a subscription, go to iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

The app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?

Please try: (1) fully closing and reopening the app, (2) restarting your device, (3) updating to the latest app version. If the problem persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I request a new feature or game?

We'd love to hear your ideas — email braingamessupport@gmail.com with your suggestion.

What devices and iOS versions are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 15.0 or later.
